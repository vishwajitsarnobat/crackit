Website Roadmap

Phase 1: Core Foundation & Admin Dashboards
- Sub-phase 1.1 (Admin/View-Only):
  - Approval Dashboard (review pending registrations)
  - Attendance Analytics (trends and breakdowns)
  - Performance Analytics (exam trends, batch comparisons, subject radars)
  - Financial Analytics (overview of expenses and salaries)
  - Profile Pages (Staff and Students, including fee details & reward points)
  - Teacher Attendance Dashboard (view teacher logs)
- Sub-phase 1.2 (Admin Data Entry):
  - Centre Management (add fields/centres)
  - User-Centre Assignments (assigning approved users)
  - Student Enrollments (handle forms and specific batch assignment)
- Sub-phase 1.3 (Centre Heads / Admin):
  - Course Management
  - Batch Management
- Sub-phase 1.4 (Cross-Role Reporting):
  - PDF & Excel report downloads for Admins, Centre Heads, and Teachers.

Phase 2: Day-to-Day Operations
- Sub-phase 2.1 (Teachers):
  - Daily Attendance Entry (Present/Absent)
  - Marks Entry (Offline scope)
  - Content Library (Upload/link PDF notes and YouTube video URLs)
- Sub-phase 2.2 (Accountants):
  - Expense Logging
  - Salary Management
  - Fee Management (generate invoices, record fee entries)
- Sub-phase 2.3 (Centre Admin):
  - Staff Attendance Entry (daily logging for teachers including late/partial)

Phase 3: Advanced Features & Engagement
- Sub-phase 3.1 (Centre Heads):
  - Reward Points Logic Implementation (Defining workflows for point awarding/deduction)
- Sub-phase 3.2 (Admins, Centre Heads, Accountants):
  - Transaction Logs Section (Immutable log of all fee and point transactions with filters)
